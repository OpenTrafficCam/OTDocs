# Usage

After assembling OTCamera on your own or receiving it from us, you can finally start using it!
Here you will find all the information you need for setting up and mounting OTCamera, recording videos and downloading them.

- [Preparation & Safety](preparation_safety.md) — charging, Wi-Fi sync, on-site checks
- [Buttons and LEDs](buttons_leds.md) — switches and LED blink patterns
- [Mounting](mounting.md) — mounting, aligning and dismounting OTCamera
- [Get the Videos](getvideos.md) — downloading videos via Wi-Fi/SSH or USB

The recorded videos can be processed further with [OTVision](../../OTVision/index.md).
